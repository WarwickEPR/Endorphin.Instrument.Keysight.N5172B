ARB

Namespace: Endorphin.Instrument.Keysight.N5172B

Nested types and modules

TypeDescription
ArbContinuousMode

The mode of a continuous trigger in the dual ARB system. "Free" means that playback begins immediately once the ARB is turned on, without waiting for a trigger, then repeats that waveform until something tells it to stop, and ignore subsequent triggers. "Trigger" does the same, but waits for an initial trigger. "Reset" is like "Trigger", but subsequent triggers reset the waveform to the beginning.
ArbExternalConnector

Physical location of the external source for the dual ARB system triggering.
ArbRetriggerMode

The behaviour of the system when a second trigger is received while in single trigger mode. "No retrigger" ignores all subsequent triggers. "Buffered retrigger" plays the segment again once it has finished. "Restart retrigger" immediately resets the segment to the beginning and starts it.
ArbSegmentAdvanceMode

The mode of a segment advance type trigger in the dual ARB system. "Single" means that on trigger, the next segment in the sequence plays once, ignoring the repetition count listed in its sequence. Sequence repetitions are NOT ignored. "Continuous" means that the segment plays in a loop until the next trigger moves it on. This also ignores the repetition count of the segment.
ArbTrigger

Complete triggering information for the dual ARB system.
ArbTriggerMode

The type of triggering to use for the dual ARB system.
ArbTriggerSource

The source to use to trigger the dual ARB system.
